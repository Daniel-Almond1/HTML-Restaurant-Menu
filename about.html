About Us

Origin Story

In the bustling heart of Bangkok, in the vibrant year of 1998, a culinary visionary named Somsak Chaiyasit opened the doors to "Siam Elegance," a Thai restaurant that would soon become a beacon of authenticity in the world of Thai cuisine. Born and raised in Thailand, Somsak brought with him a deep passion for the rich tapestry of flavors that define the country's culinary heritage. The original location, nestled in a historic building adorned with traditional Thai art, quickly gained acclaim for its commitment to using genuine Thai ingredients and time-honored recipes, transporting diners to the bustling streets of Bangkok with every bite. As Siam Elegance's reputation flourished, so did its reach, evolving into a beloved chain of restaurants across the globe, each preserving the essence of Thai culinary tradition under Somsak's watchful eye.
